About Strap2

Few more words about straps.

They are both 16mm wide, as they should be, non-synthetic, and it was really hard to find over the whole world ....

they went a long way to get to me. The slider buckle and tips weren't found on the market, so it was necessary to make a blueprint, order metal parts, and separately stamps.

All assembled and painted by my hands, maybe not perfect - but as it is. All threads are not synthetic too. All stitching is done by hand.

Each strap takes a few hours of my time and the price will be definitely bigger for all next.
